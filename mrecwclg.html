About College

Malla Reddy Engineering College for Women (MRECW) is a premier institution dedicated to empowering women through quality technical education. The college fosters innovation, leadership, and excellence in engineering and technology.

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

About Department: The ECE department at MRECW specializes in communication systems, VLSI design, embedded systems, and signal processing. It fosters innovation through practical exposure and technical workshops.
